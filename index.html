Welcome to CampDAO

Over the last week, members of the EOS community got together for the 2nd annual BP Summit in San Francisco. And within the EOS community, getting together is always an opportunity to have fun and build things. Pioneered by community members, we'd like to introduce CampDAO.

  • Burning Man

    Burning Man is an annual nine-day gathering in the Nevada Desert (USA). It is a one of a kind festival, a big decentralized community that believes in collaboration over competition.

    • Anyone may be a part of Burning Man.
    • Burning Man is devoted to acts of gift giving. The value of a gift is unconditional.
    • Burning Man values creative cooperation, collaboration and interaction.
    • Everyone is invited to work. Everyone is invited to play.

    The majority of people who go to Burning Man are part of planned, registered camps. Burning Man, in esssence, is decentralized collective of communities. Likewise, we aim to run CampDAO in a decentralized and cooperative way.

  • EOSIO

    EOSIO is a decentralized blockchain infrastructure that emulates the attributes of computer hardware, including CPUs, RAM, and storage. The system is powered by the EOS coin, whose holders can access the distributed computing resources.

    • EOSIO is the most used blockchain software in the world.
    • The architecture designed to enable scaling of decentralized applications.
    • With EOSIO, users can interact with the blockchain without having to know how it works.

    By using EOSIO to run CampDAO, this project is a chance to experiment with and showcase how blockchain tools can be used to organize communities and events in a decentralized way.

  • CampDAO

    CampDAO is an opportunity to build relationships into other like-minded communities, strengthen the relationships within the EOS community, and to start a tradition that can grow and thrive by the will of the CampDAO hivemind.

    • CampDAO tokens represent voting rights in operating decisions.
    • CampDAO tokens can be earned by contributing to CampDAO.
    • Members will be sent to CampDAO by holding a vote amongst CampDAO holders.
    • Anyone can contribute to the project.
    • Voting power will be proportional to contributions.

How Will CampDAO Work?

CampDAO will be managed and organized using DACfactory by eosDAC. Thanks @LukeStokes for helping out!

Tentative CampDAO guidelines

  • CampDAO tokens represent voting rights in operating decisions.
  • CampDAO tokens can be earned by contributing to CampDAO.
  • Members will be sent to CampDAO by holding a vote amongst CampDAO holders.
  • Voting power will be proportional to contributions.
  • The camp size and number of attendees will depend on the amount raised.
  • At least ⅓ of the camp's participants should be veterans to Burning Man.
  • External members can be accepted into the community through vouching.
  • Vouching requires KYC or known by someone who is in the initial CampDAO group.
  • Vouching for someone means you take responsibility for them.
  • There will be a record for who vouched for whom.
  • 10% of all tickets go to the ticket lottery. If you contribute with at least 10 EOS, you can participate in the lottery. You can buy as many lottery tickets (costing 10 EOS each) as you want.
  • Lottery tickets are consumed every year.
  • If tickets can’t be acquired or the token distribution fails to reach the minimum amount, funds are returned to contributors.
  • If you arrange your own ticket before joining the CampDao, you get rewarded the equivalent of $1200 in CampDAO Votes upon joining the cam.
  • If CampDAO sends you to the Burn, you must burn some of your CampDAO tokens (TBD).

Sale

Sale starts on June 24th and goes until July 1st. Dutch auction for a total of 10,000 tokens. The minimum goal for the fundraise to start out the project is 10,000 EOS in 2019. If the goal fails to meet, the raised funds will be refunded to the original account. The purpose of the tokens are to participate as a CampDAO voter on all aspects of managing CampDAO that are put to a vote. Initial funds raised will be used to invest in camp costs.
